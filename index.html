Election Timeline

Key milestones for the General Elections 2024 — scroll to view all dates.

  1. ✓ Completed 28 March 2024

    Registration Deadline

    Last date to submit Form 6 on the NVSP portal to register as a new voter for Phase 1 constituencies.

    📋 Form 6 🌐 NVSP Portal
  2. ✓ Completed 16 Mar – 30 May 2024

    Campaign Period

    Candidates and parties campaign across constituencies. Campaign activities cease 48 hours before polling in each phase.

    📢 Model Code Active ⏱ 48hr Silence Rule
  3. ⚡ Ongoing 19 Apr – 1 Jun 2024

    Voting Day (7 Phases)

    Voters cast ballots at their assigned polling booths. Polling hours are 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Valid photo ID required.

    🕖 7AM – 6PM 🏛 7 Phases 🪪 Photo ID Required
  4. Upcoming 4 June 2024

    Result Day

    Counting of votes begins at 8:00 AM simultaneously across all constituencies. Results declared by evening.

    📊 Vote Count 📺 Live Updates
